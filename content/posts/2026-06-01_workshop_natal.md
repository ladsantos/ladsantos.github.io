I am briefly advertising the open call for abstracts and financial support to attend this excellent workshop organized by Adriana Valio and colleagues (including myself as part of the Scientific Organizing Committee).

> We invite researchers and students to submit abstracts for contributed talks and poster presentations for the international workshop [“From Stars to Life: Stellar Impacts on Planetary Atmospheres, Habitability, and Biosignatures Detection”](https://sites.google.com/view/fromstarstolife/home) hosted at the International Institute of Physics (IIP) in Natal, Brazil, from **November 2–6, 2026**. This event will convene astronomers, astrobiologists, and planetary scientists to explore the critical links between stellar activity, exoplanets atmospheres, and the potential for life on distant worlds.

Abstract submission: [Use this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScWtgqfhgRfEiGASZmlV3AS8uF4z5XHuJ3576twF9YMkqhatg/viewform) to submit your abstract

Deadline: **June 26 (11:59pm UTC-3)**

> Limited funding is available to support the participation of students and early-career researchers. Grants may cover registration fees, travel, or accommodation.

Grant Application Link: [Apply for Financial Support](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd8aHiT_xxa8D-Z8cQOGTkIs-RZBevSaUUB9gTPpSmh615DAA/viewform)

Deadline: **June 26 (11:59pm UTC-3)**
